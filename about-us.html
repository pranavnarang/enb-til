About the Lab

" We build advanced technology products to deliver energy in a safer and more reliable way to fuel a rapidly innovating world. "

- T+IL Lab Mission Statement

Employee Value Proposition

Working hands-on with cutting edge technology, the lab is pioneering the development of innovative products through small agile teams. Lab talent is exposed to a variety of multidisciplinary skills, including industrial predictive algorithms, machine learning, and sentiment analysis.

By working in the Lab, opportunities for career progression in people leadership and/or technical expertise go hand in hand with a work is play environment where a flexible fun and modern working style is offered to support individual needs.
