Terminal Optimization

Terminal optimization is one of several ways that Enbridge is optimizing the world’s longest and most complex crude oil and liquids network—supporting the energy sector by getting Canadian crude oil to market, and helping Canadians reap the benefits of a natural resources economy.

GTM Pipeline Integrity is a project that allows Enbridge to continuously drive improvements to pipeline integrity and safety by bringing multiple field monitoring and assessment data in one place, and analyzing where engineers need to pay attention.

Idea Intake Process

The Technology + Innovation Lab will act as the delivery engine for agile teams to design, build, and deploy products with the biggest impact for our business.Innovation ideas come to the Lab from the Business, and the Lab Executive Team arbitrates whether those ideas should be pursued in the Lab. Business Relation Manager's (BRM's) will collaborate with the business to develop an idea prior to submission into the lab for consideration. You can read about the intake lab process more here.
